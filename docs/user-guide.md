# Hearst Connect — User Guide

## What is Hearst Connect?

Hearst Connect is an institutional DeFi platform offering the **Hearst Yield Vault** — a single-vault structured yield product backed by Bitcoin mining operations.

- **Target APY range**: 8–15% (always displayed as a range, never a single point)
- **Currency**: USDC monthly distributions
- **Structure**: Cayman Exempted Limited Partnership
- **Minimum ticket**: $250,000
- **Lock-up**: 60-day soft lock-up

## Dashboard

The Dashboard is your real-time view of the vault's health.

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Description | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **AUM** | Assets Under Management in USDC | Live |
| **APY Range** | Projected annual yield (low-high) | Computed |
| **Stressed APY** | Worst-case scenario yield | Estimated |
| **Risk Score** | Composite 0-100 score | Partial |
| **Mining Margin** | Health of mining operations | Attested |

### Allocation Buckets

The vault deploys capital across 4 buckets:

1. **Mining Cashflow** (~35%) — Revenue-share Bitcoin mining contracts
2. **BTC Tactical** (~15%) — Rule-based Bitcoin exposure (accumulate/take profit)
3. **USDC Base Yield** (~40%) — Lending protocols (Aave, Morpho, Sky)
4. **Stable Reserve** (~10%) — T-bill backed stablecoins

### BTC Tactical Sleeve

Displays current Bitcoin exposure with:
- Position size (USD and % AUM)
- P&L vs average entry ($58,420)
- Next triggers (accumulate below -20%, take profit above +30%)
- Guardrails (volatility, mining margin, concentration, liquidity)

## Scenario Lab

The Scenario Lab lets you simulate vault performance under different market conditions.

### Parameters

- **BTC Price Change** (-100% to +300%)
- **Hashprice** ($/TH/day) — mining revenue per terahash
- **Energy Cost** ($/kWh) — hosting electricity cost
- **Stable APY** (0-30%) — yield from stablecoin lending
- **Volatility Index** (0-100) — BTC 30-day volatility

### Presets

| Preset | Description |
|---|---|
| **Base** | Neutral market conditions |
| **BTC Bear** | BTC -40%, hashprice compression |
| **BTC Bull** | BTC +60%, hashprice expansion |
| **Mining Compression** | Hashprice -25%, energy cost +15% |
| **Extreme Stress** | BTC -50%, hashprice -40%, high volatility |

### Outputs

Each scenario produces:
- APY range (low-high)
- Stressed APY
- Risk score
- Mining margin score
- Allocation breakdown
- BTC tactical assessment

## Investor Memo

The Investor Memo is an institutional-grade report generated by the Kimi K2.6 LLM (via Hypercli).

### Sections

1. **Executive Summary** — High-level vault performance and posture
2. **Vault Structure** — Cayman SPV mechanics, terms, lock-up
3. **Scenario Analysis** — All scenarios with APY ranges and PTAI format
4. **Risk Section** — 5 canonical risks (market, mining, liquidity, smart contract, counterparty)
5. **Mining Section** — Hashrate, margin, energy, uptime context
6. **Performance Section** — Backtest summaries with drawdown context
7. **Methodology Note** — Version, data sources, confidence scoring
8. **Disclaimer** — Regulatory disclaimer (verbatim)

### Important Notes

- The memo is **not auto-distributed** — it only leaves when you download it
- Every section cites at least one assumption
- APY is always a range, never a single point
- Forbidden words are automatically rejected ("guarantee", "promise", "risk-free")

## Methodology

All projections follow **Hearst Methodology v1.0**.

### Key Principles

1. **APY always as range** — Low-high, never single point
2. **Deterministic rule-based** — No Monte Carlo simulation
3. **PTAI format** — Every action: Projection → Trigger → Action → Impact
4. **Assumption-driven** — Every projection explicitly states its assumptions
5. **Forbidden language** — No unconditional guarantees

### Rebalancing Rules

The vault rebalances based on:
- Risk score thresholds (defensive/balanced/opportunistic modes)
- Mining margin compression
- BTC drawdowns and volatility spikes
- Concentration limits

## FAQ

**Q: Is the APY guaranteed?**
A: No. APY ranges are projections based on stated assumptions, not guarantees. Past performance does not predict future results.

**Q: What happens if Bitcoin price crashes?**
A: The vault shifts to "defensive" mode, reducing mining and BTC tactical exposure, increasing stable reserves.

**Q: How often is the memo generated?**
A: On-demand via the Investor Memo page, plus automatically on the 1st of each month at 09:00 UTC.

**Q: Can I withdraw anytime?**
A: The vault has a 60-day soft lock-up. Requests are processed at the next distribution window.

**Q: Where are the mining operations?**
A: Hosted at partner facilities. Attestations are published in the Proof Center.
