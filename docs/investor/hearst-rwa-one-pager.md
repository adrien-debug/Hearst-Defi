# Hearst Yield Vault — Institutional One-Pager

> **Canonical positioning. Read this before writing any LP-facing copy, memo, deck, or legal text.**

## One-liner
**Institutional RWA Yield Vault backed by Bitcoin Mining Cash Flows.**

## 30-second pitch
Hearst Yield Vault deploys qualified-investor capital into a Model B structure: principal is held in a **USDC reserve** (not deployed on-chain), and a **monthly USDC distribution** is funded by a **Bitcoin mining revenue-share**. A tokenized USDC / T-bills base sleeve provides stable yield; a **small capped BTC tactical sleeve** adds cycle convexity. The product is governed by a distribution-coverage discipline that prioritizes **stability of distribution and preservation of capital**. Target net range 8–15% (not guaranteed).

## 5-minute LP explanation
1. **Why mining yield is institutional** — Bitcoin mining is a predictable cash-generation business; net margin = hashprice − energy − difficulty drag, scaled by uptime.
2. **Model B mechanics** — principal sits in a USDC reserve; yield is a mining-revenue-share injected monthly. No on-chain strategy execution.
3. **Distribution coverage governs** — `coverage = net mining cash flow ÷ target monthly distribution`. Coverage drives both distribution and core allocation; distributions are reduced/suspended before principal is eroded.
4. **BTC is a factor, not the promise** — Bitcoin price acts through hashprice (revenue) and a capped satellite sleeve; it is not the primary exposure.
5. **Proof** — mining revenue attestations + Fireblocks custody proof-of-reserves + on-chain event log.

## What Hearst IS
- An **RWA income vehicle** whose engine is **mining cash generation → distribution coverage**.
- A **Cayman SPV**, qualified investors only, $250k min, 60-day soft lock-up, monthly USDC distributions.
- A **capital-preservation-first** product (income, not a directional crypto bet).

## What Hearst IS NOT
- ❌ a DeFi protocol / yield farm (Aave, Compound).
- ❌ a Bitcoin fund / speculative crypto product.
- ❌ an APY-first product without backing cash flow.
- ❌ a generic crypto vault.
