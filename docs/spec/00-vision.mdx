# Hearst Connect — Vision

## What it is

A single institutional USDC vault — **Hearst Yield Vault** — combining three yield engines in one envelope:

1. **Bitcoin Mining Cashflow** (30–40% AUM) — revenue-share with farm(s), distributed in USDC monthly.
2. **USDC Yield Base** (25–60% AUM) — T-bills tokenisées (Ondo) + lending stable (Maple/Aave).
3. **BTC Tactical** (0–30% AUM) — accumulation at low prices, profit-taking at high prices, sized by rules.

## Headline target

**APY range 8–15%**, reference point ~12%, always presented with stressed APY visible.
Distributions: **monthly in USDC**.
Minimum ticket: **$250k**.
Soft lock-up: **60 days**.
Fees: **1% mgmt + 10% perf** (high-watermark).
Structure: **Cayman Exempted Limited Partnership (ELP)**, pro investors only.

## Three product promises

1. **Lisibility** — a LP must understand the strategy in 5 minutes.
2. **Simulability** — every yield assumption must be stressable interactively.
3. **Auditability** — proof of reserves, smart contract events, published methodology.

## What we are NOT

- Not a multi-vault platform (single product at MVP).
- Not a quant terminal (no VaR/CVaR/Sharpe headline).
- Not retail.
- Not a DeFi protocol — we are a **manager** consuming protocols.

## The 4 MVP screens

1. **Dashboard** — what the vault is today
2. **Scenario Lab** — what the vault becomes tomorrow under stated assumptions
3. **Proof Center** — why the numbers can be trusted
4. **Investor Memo** — 8-page PDF generated in one click
