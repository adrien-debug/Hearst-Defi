# Glossary

## Product

- **Hearst Yield Vault** — the single MVP product. Cayman ELP, $250k min, 60d soft lock.
- **AUM** — Assets Under Management. Total USDC equivalent.
- **APY range** — forward 12m projected APY low and high, never a single point.
- **Stressed APY** — APY under combined Bear + Mining Compression scenario.
- **Distribution** — monthly USDC payout to LPs.

## Mining

- **Hashprice** — revenue per unit of hashrate per day, in USD. Source: Luxor / Hashrate Index.
- **Hashrate** — computing power, TH/s (terahashes/second).
- **Difficulty** — network parameter; adjusts every 2016 blocks (~2 weeks).
- **Mining margin** — net mining revenue after operating costs.
- **Mining Margin Score** — 0–100 composite (see `05-mining-model.mdx`).
- **Operational Confidence** — 0–100 partner reliability score.
- **Revenue-share** — Hearst's % of partner farm gross margin.

## Vault

- **Regime** — one of Defensive / Balanced / Opportunistic. Hard bounds enforced on-chain.
- **PTAI** — Projection → Trigger → Action → Impact. The standard format for any simulation or action.
- **Withdrawal queue** — 60-day soft lock-up queue (Phase 3+).

## Data provenance badges

- **Live** — fresh on-chain or oracle.
- **Oracle** — aggregated oracle feed (e.g., Chainlink).
- **Attested** — signed by partner (mining attestation, custodian).
- **Estimated** — derived from live inputs by the engine.
- **Manual** — manual hypothesis (e.g., scenario input).
- **Stale** — beyond freshness threshold.

## Methodology

- **Methodology v1.0** — immutable spec document for projections. Versioned; bumps require ADR.
- **Confidence** — Low / Medium / High score for any projection. Function of data freshness, historical fit, assumption range, rebalancing room.

## Structure

- **Cayman ELP** — Cayman Exempted Limited Partnership. The fund structure.
- **GP** — General Partner (Hearst Management Co).
- **LP** — Limited Partner (the investor).
- **Multisig** — Safe 3/5 wallet that approves rebalancing.
- **Timelock** — 48h delay on critical contract changes.
